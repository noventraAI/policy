Delete Account Policy

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email request to:
noventraai2025@gmail.com

2. Important:
The deletion request must be sent from the same email address associated with your account in our system. This is to ensure we can verify your identity and protect your data from unauthorized deletion requests.

3. What happens next:
Once we receive your request, we will verify the email address. After verification, we will permanently delete your account and all related data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

4. Note:
Deletion is irreversible. After your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover any data, content, or access previously tied to your account.
